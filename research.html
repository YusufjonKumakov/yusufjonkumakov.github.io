PhD Thesis Focus

Developing ML‑enhanced workflows for time‑lapse (4D) seismic inversion of reservoir pressure and saturation. Emphasis on physics‑aware modeling and integration of reservoir engineering constraints for robust, decision‑grade estimates.

  • Data‑driven inversion with physics/constraints
  • Time‑lapse consistency and uncertainty awareness
  • Applications to CO₂ storage and hydrocarbon fields
