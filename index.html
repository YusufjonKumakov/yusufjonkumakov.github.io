About

I am a Postgraduate Research Student (PhD) in GeoEnergy Engineering at Heriot‑Watt University, specialising in 4D seismic inversion, machine learning, and subsurface reservoir monitoring. My work combines physics‑based geophysical theory with modern data‑driven approaches to improve time‑lapse (4D) seismic interpretation for hydrocarbon reservoirs and CO₂ storage sites.

I have experience as a Chief Geophysics Specialist, Assistant Lecturer, and Junior Researcher, contributing to real‑world geophysical studies, reservoir modelling, and subsurface uncertainty analysis. I aim to build reliable, reproducible ML‑augmented inversion workflows that deliver decision‑grade subsurface insights.
