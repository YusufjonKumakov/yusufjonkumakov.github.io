About

I am a Postgraduate Research Student (PhD) in GeoEnergy Engineering at Heriot‑Watt University, specialising in 4D seismic inversion, machine learning, and subsurface reservoir monitoring. I integrate physics‑based geophysical methods with modern data‑driven approaches to improve time‑lapse (4D) seismic interpretation for hydrocarbon reservoirs and CO₂ storage sites.

My background covers seismic interpretation, inversion workflow design, reservoir modelling, and ML/DL for geoscience. I focus on building reliable, reproducible pipelines that deliver decision‑grade subsurface insights.
