About

I am a PhD Researcher at Heriot-Watt University specialising in 4D seismic inversion, machine learning, and subsurface reservoir monitoring. My background spans geophysics, reservoir engineering, and deep learning, with industry experience in seismic interpretation and hydrocarbon reserves analysis.

I am passionate about integrating physics-based methods with modern AI/ML to improve time‑lapse (4D) seismic inversion for CO₂ storage and hydrocarbon fields.
