About

I am a PhD student in GeoEnergy Engineering at Heriot‑Watt University, focusing on 4D seismic inversion, machine learning, and subsurface reservoir monitoring. I integrate geophysical and reservoir engineering constraints to improve inversion accuracy for hydrocarbon and CO₂ storage applications.
